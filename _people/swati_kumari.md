**Bio**<br>
[Dr. Swati Kumari](https://experts.deakin.edu.au/67171-swati-kumari) is a Senior Lecturer in Cybersecurity at [Deakin University, GIFT City, India](https://www.deakin.edu.au/about-deakin/locations/campuses/gift-city-india) from June 2024. Before joining Deakin, she was a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at [Trinity College Dublin, Ireland](https://www.tcd.ie/), in the School of Computer Science and Statistics. Her research expertise spans **IoT Security, Post-Quantum Cryptography, Federated Learning, and Blockchain Technology**.  

With over **14 years of experience** across industry, research, and academia—including her tenure at the [Thapar Institute of Engineering & Technology](https://thapar.edu/)—Dr. Swati brings extensive multidisciplinary knowledge to her academic role. She earned her **Ph.D. in Computer Science** from Thapar Institute of Engineering & Technology, Patiala, India.  

Dr. Swati’s scholarly contributions include multiple publications in reputed **SCI-indexed journals and international conferences**. Her current research focuses on **NIST-standardized post-quantum algorithms**, with recent projects exploring **IoT security using lattice-based cryptography**, **blockchain consensus mechanisms employing FALCON**, and **enhancing TLS security with CRYSTALS-Kyber**.
